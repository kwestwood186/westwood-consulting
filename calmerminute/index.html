About & privacy

Pause guides you through public-domain calming techniques — breathwork, CBT reframing, grounding, muscle relaxation, and mindful resets — adapted for short, discreet use at work.

Your privacy: everything happens on this device. Anything you type is held only in memory and is cleared the moment you leave an exercise. Nothing is sent to a server or saved to your history.
