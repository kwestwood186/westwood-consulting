What I speak on

Career services & workforce development

What's actually working in modern employment services, the trends reshaping career counselling, and how frontline staff can deliver better outcomes for clients.

Accessibility & accommodations

Helping job seekers with disabilities navigate the workplace, assistive technology that levels the playing field, and how employers can build truly inclusive hiring practices.

Job search strategy

The resume vs. cover letter distinction, interview preparation, salary negotiation, and the mindset shift that separates candidates who get offers from those who don't.

Frontline staff development
