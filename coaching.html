What we'll cover

Every session is tailored to where you are in your job search. Most clients use their first session for one of these:

Resume & cover letter review

I'll go through your application materials line by line, flagging what's working, what's killing your chances, and what to rewrite.

Interview preparation

Mock interview with the specific questions you're likely to get. Real-time feedback on your answers, body language, and what to ask.

Salary negotiation

We'll do the research together, set your anchor number, work out your "no number," and rehearse the actual conversation.

Career transition strategy

Switching industries or moving into a new function? We'll map your transferable skills and design a job search plan that actually works.

Application strategy

How to tailor each application, when to use referrals, when to skip the portal and reach out directly. Build a system, not a spray.

Anything else

Got a specific question or situation? Send it in the pre-call questionnaire and we'll spend the full hour on it.

FAQ

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem — let me know at least 24 hours before your session and we'll move it. Less than 24 hours notice forfeits the session fee, with one exception per client for emergencies.

Do you offer packages or multi-session discounts?

Not yet — every session is currently a standalone 60-minute booking. If you want ongoing coaching across a multi-month job search, email me and we'll work something out.

What if I'm not sure coaching is right for me?

Start with the free guides on this site. They cover the same frameworks I use in coaching. If you work through them and still feel stuck on something specific, that's exactly when coaching is most valuable.

Can you guarantee I'll get a job?

No — and you should be suspicious of anyone who does. What I can do is dramatically improve the quality of your applications, your interview performance, and your negotiation outcomes. The rest is on you and the market.

Where are you based, and do you work with clients outside Canada?

I'm based in Hamilton, Ontario. Sessions are by video call, so location doesn't matter — I work with clients across Canada and the U.S. The job search principles apply globally, but specific salary research and labour-market data will be tailored to your region.
